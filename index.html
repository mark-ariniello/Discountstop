About us

Eliminate the receipt drawer

Receipts can provide valuable data about the items you buy but most people don't bother to take them or ever use that information. With Receipt Delete all of that information is stored digitally.

Organize Data

Have an itemized list of the items that you buy displayed, in a multitudue of ways so that you can see when and where you spend money, and identify ways to save.

Protect yourself from impulse buys

Use the data gathered and displayed by Receipt Delete to make sure that you are not continuously falling victim to impluse buys on every shopping trip.

Identify ways to save

By using the information that is contained in your receipts you can see whether items you normally buy are going up or down in value, and plan accordingly. You can also see if you buy the same things over and over again, and try something new instead.

"the Quantified Self"

Right now, we’re in the middle of a revolution. The “Quantified Self” revolution. Now, more than ever, people want more and more quantitative information about themselves and their lives.

So what are we missing? What information are we ignoring simply because it’s inconvenient?

What about what we buy, but when we buy, how we buy, and who we buy from?

Our app, Receipt Delete, allows you to simply take a picture of your receipt, and track all of that data

If you have the data, you can understand your purchases, and what makes you likely to impulse buy so you can avoid those triggers. So track your receipts, learn more about yourself, and join the revolution.
